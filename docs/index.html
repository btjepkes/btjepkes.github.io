Hello! My name is Ben Tjepkes and I am a wildlife ecologist and geographer who loves data.

This is my online portfolio showcasing my professional and creative works. I am currently a graduate student at the University of Missouri studying geographic information systems in the Department of Geography. My work focuses on using a variety of data science and geospatial tools to conduct research in ecology and biogeography. My interests span the fields of geospatial, ecology, data science, and climate science. Please have a look around at my blog posts, research overviews, visualization gallery, full curriculum vitae, and lots more.
