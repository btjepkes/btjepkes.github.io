Beaver-Impacted Water Control Structures

Assessing the need for flow devices on wetlands in NE Wisconsin.

Created by:
Benjamin Tjepkes

This undergraduate research project was aimed at assessing the need for beaver flow devices on water control strutures owned by the Wisconsin Department of Natural Resources. From these results, I designed a specialized flow device for the structures most impacted by beavers in the study area.
